Human Scenarios

Public Transit

Silent Panic Events

When a passenger freezes due to a medical episode or harassment, SSL identifies the lack of natural movement and erratic breathing patterns.

Crowd Safety

Stampede Prevention

SSL monitors crowd rhythm. A sudden change in movement cadence combined with high-distress facial signals triggers early warning.

Domestic Emergency

Elderly Welfare

A fall without a call. SSL detects the sudden cessation of movement followed by micro-vocalizations of distress, alerting care teams.

Privacy as a Pre-requisite

SSL does not recognize faces. It does not record conversations. It only processes the geometry of distress.

Edge Analysis

Data is processed locally on the sensor. Only signal intensity and confidence scores are transmitted to dispatch.

Pattern Masking

Identity-revealing features are blurred or omitted at the hardware level. The engine only "sees" points of motion.

Human in the Loop

SSL suggests; it never commands. Final response authority always rests with human dispatch.
